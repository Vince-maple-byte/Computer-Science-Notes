[[List of html elements]]

The headings in html are made up of 6 different variants with 1 being the largest and 6 being the smallest
Headings formatted example:
```
<h6>Your Heading</h6>
<h5>Your Heading</h5>
<h4>Your Heading</4>
<h3>Your Heading</h3>
<h2>Your Heading</h2>
<h1>Your Heading</h1>

```
